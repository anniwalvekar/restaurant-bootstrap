About us

The menu of Spice Kitchen is a varied one, covering a multitude of cuisines from all over the world. Italian, Asian, North Indian, South Indian and European, all are available here and cooked to perfection. Dining here doesn’t come cheap so be prepared. Keep an eye open for their buffets, which you will find to be lavish as well as economical. Breakfast is quite a thing of beauty, don’t skip.

The restaurant is generously spacious with formal dining tables added to it for proper sit-down meal. It is cheerfully lit with bright lights and there is plenty of space to sit around comfortably. Spice Kitchen is ideal for a fail-safe dinner with loved ones.
